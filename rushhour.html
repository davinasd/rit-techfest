Rush Hour

Defusing bombs and infusing stress

11th Nov

Venue: -

Team Size: 3-4 Members

Registration Fee: -

Overview:

  • A time bomb will explode when its countdown timer reaches 0:00. The participants should decode the 5, 6 clues provided and obtain passwords to defuse the bomb. The team that defuses the bomb in least time will be declared the winner.

Rules:

  • - The passwords will have alpha-numeric values that must be identified by the participants, by the set of clues provided.
  • - The participants will be given a riddles, crosswords etc., which has to be decoded by them and the answer to the riddle gives different passwords.
  • - The clues can be general and/or related to Electronics, Electrical and Computer Networks domain.
  • - Finally, the passwords must be combined and rearranged if necessary to defuse the time bomb.
  • - Participants must finish the tasks within the allotted time.
  • - The teams will be given points based on the time taken to complete the tasks for each clue.
  • - The team with the highest cumulative score after all the clues will be declared as the winner of the event.
