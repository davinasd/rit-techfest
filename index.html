About RIT Techfest 2023

Ramaiah Institute of Technology's annual Technical Fest is finally here. Excited?

It is themed on the Multiverse, comprising hands-on events, competitions, and fun activities. A 2 day collegiate technical fest organized by RIT-Bangalore. Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT), founded in 1962, offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in a wide range of engineering disciplines. RIT is known for its rigorous academic curriculum, experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant extracurricular activities, which prepare students for success in their careers and in life.
